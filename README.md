# SongCatcher.io 🎵    (Dev in progress)

> Hear the beat. Catch the song. Beat the world.

SongCatcher.io is a fast-paced multiplayer music guessing game where players compete in real-time by identifying songs from tiny audio snippets.

Challenge friends, climb global leaderboards, discover legendary tracks, and prove your music knowledge across generations, genres, and artists.

---

# 🌟 What is SongCatcher.io?

SongCatcher.io transforms music trivia into a live social gaming experience.

Every round starts with a very short clip of a song. If nobody guesses correctly, the clip gets slightly longer each time — giving players more chances to identify the track.

The faster you guess, the more points you earn.

Whether you're a Bollywood fanatic, a retro music lover, or someone who knows every Taylor Swift intro in 2 seconds — there’s a mode for everyone.

# ⏱️ Progressive Audio Challenge

Songs begin with extremely short snippets.

If nobody guesses correctly, the clip duration increases.

| Round | Clip Duration |
|---|---|
| 1️⃣ | 2 seconds |
| 2️⃣ | 3 seconds |
| 3️⃣ | 5 seconds |
| 4️⃣ | 8 seconds |

### Gameplay Rules

- Unlimited guesses
- Faster guesses earn more points
- Live scoreboards update instantly
- Competitive scoring inspired by Scribble.io
